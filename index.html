About

To look at our world, look at how we can protect her, to make people smarter, to provide a collection point for examples of simulations, collect data and like minds together.

Meetups

Join us! Meet fellow adventurers, environmentalists, humanists, hackers and artists. Make models of whole ecosystems including human communities. Simulate outcomes and try new ideas. Share your projects and ideas. Help explore the possibilities of the future of our life on Earth.

Projects

Projects that people in our community make are posted here. All of our simulations are open so that anybody can play with them, change the data and test different outcomes based on different assumptions. We're also especially interested in whole systems models of entire watersheds and communities that explore the intersection of land use, law and policy with an eye on long term sustainability.
